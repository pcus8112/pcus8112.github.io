Our approach

Renschler Editions combines books and practical educational tools under one roof.

The publishing house is international. At the same time, it follows a clear internal order: subjects are presented with calm structure, understandable language and a visible respect for the reader.

Our work is created with a particular sensitivity toward women, while remaining open to anyone who values clarity, substance and a more thoughtful tone.

History

Philosophy

Philosophical work at Renschler Editions is meant to remain readable, clear and serious without becoming cold or empty.

Gerald Renschler

Gerald Renschler is the author and publisher behind Renschler Editions.

His work connects books, educational ideas and structured tools. The goal is not only to publish content, but to build a coherent system in which books, language, thought and practical learning aids support one another.

This is why Renschler Editions is not limited to one type of publication. It includes books, downloadable materials and EVDE as a specialised line for guided Excel tools.

Publishing channels

The publishing structure is intentionally kept clear by language:

German books are published via BoD.

English and French books are published via Amazon Kindle.

EVDE tools are linked across all language versions of the site, with a clear note that they are currently available in German only.
