About

Maison Singé Éditions is the publishing house of the Maison Singé label.

Maison Singé stands for transparent luxury. Its jewellery is deliberately gold-plated rather than made of solid gold. This distinction is communicated openly. The price reflects the value of real gold, as the difference between material value and retail price is equally dedicated to humanitarian aid in Africa and to animal protection and animal rights initiatives.

Maison Singé Éditions extends this philosophy into the cultural sphere. The publishing house releases works in three curated editorial lines:

histoire — history and historical analysis
esprit — religion, philosophy and spiritual traditions
imaginaires — literature, creative works and experimental forms

The publishing house positions itself as an independent platform for thought, memory and imagination.
