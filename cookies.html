Privacy and simplicity

No cookies? Why we are different.

Many websites ask for permission before you can even read them. We chose a quieter and simpler path.

Gerald Renschler

Author & Publisher

A conscious decision

Many websites today display cookie banners because they use tracking, advertising networks or analytics systems that collect visitor data.

Renschler Editions deliberately takes a different approach.

We want this website to feel readable, calm and respectful from the first moment. That includes restraint in the way technology is used.

What this means in practice

  • No advertising networks
  • No tracking of visitor behaviour
  • No analytics tools for marketing purposes
  • No unnecessary data collection for reading this website

In short: this website is meant to work without surveillance.

Why this matters

Privacy should not feel like a luxury feature. It should feel normal.

A publishing house that wants to present books, ideas and educational materials does not need to turn every visitor into a data point.

That is why you do not see a cookie banner here.
