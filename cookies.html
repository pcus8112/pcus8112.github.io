No cookies? Why we are different.

Many websites today display cookie notices.

This is because they collect visitor data — for advertising, tracking or usage analytics.

We consciously refrain from doing so.

This website:

– uses no tracking
– stores no personal data
– uses no advertising networks
– analyzes no user behavior

It works without surveillance.

We believe privacy is not a luxury, but something natural.

That is why you do not see a cookie banner here.

Welcome to a website you can simply read.
